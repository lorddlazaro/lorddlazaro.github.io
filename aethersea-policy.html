⚠ Inactive Member Removal

Members removed due to inactivity are not permanently blacklisted. Since we are primarily recruiting active GvG players, inactive members may be removed to free up roster space. Removed members are welcome to reapply at any time. Applications will be considered when guild slots become available, though leadership may also make room for returning members at its discretion.

📋 Removal Thresholds by Rank

Rank Active GvG Player Non-GvG Player
Core Disciple / Hall Master 4 weeks inactive (even with notice)
2 weeks inactive (without notice)		 Below 4,000 Monthly Activity Points

Outer Disciple / Apprentice 1 week inactive (even with notice) Below 2,500 Weekly Activity Points

⚔ To be considered an Active GvG Player:

Guild War Signups

Members are expected to vote on every Guild War signup in #guild-war-signups. Make sure to vote on both Saturday and Sunday.

  • Voting Absent is perfectly fine if you cannot attend.
  • Must sign up as attending and actually attend if rostered at least 5 games in the last 2 weeks.

📅 Extended Absences

If you will be unavailable for more than 7 days, post in #inactivity-notice beforehand so we can take it into consideration.

👑 Leadership Discretion

The inactivity policy serves as a guideline, not an automatic removal system. Meeting any removal condition does not guarantee an immediate kick. Leadership may consider factors such as:

  • Prior contributions
  • Communication
  • Attendance history
  • Guild needs
  • Exceptional circumstances

Likewise, leadership reserves the right to make exceptions or retain members who would otherwise qualify for removal when deemed beneficial for the guild.

📜 Policy Disclaimer

These policies are subject to change at any time as the needs of the guild evolve. Leadership reserves the right to modify, add, remove, or make exceptions to any policy when deemed necessary for the guild's best interests.
